Feature Overview – FAQs

1. What is Synchro and who is it for?
Synchro is a workspace collaboration tool for teachers, admins, and delegates (students), designed to streamline communication, academic progress tracking, and resource sharing.
2. What are the main features for Admins?
Admins can create workspaces, assign semester objectives, manage teacher tasks, and track progress through visual dashboards and reports.
3. What can Teachers do on Synchro?
Teachers upload files, log lecture hours, assign homework using Google Forms, and communicate with delegates and admins.
4. What do Delegates gain from Synchro?
Delegates (students) can access course files, track assignments, receive notifications, and stay updated with academic progress through their dashboard.
5. Does Synchro support messaging?
Yes, Synchro integrates Gmail API to enable secure, role-based messaging between teachers, delegates, and admins.
